# PymeOS installed

PymeOS is now installed in your private Forger space. It stores your Pyme's data on your computer and opens as its own app.

What this version (0.1.0) includes:

- employee records and contracts
- payslip structure
- vacation tracking
- Previred export structure
- Chilean legal parameters loaded from a versioned file

This is an initial version: the structure is in place, but automatic payslip calculation and Previred file generation are not implemented yet. You can start by registering your employees and their contracts.

Suggested first request: tell me how many people work with you and I will help you register the first record.
